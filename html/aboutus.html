Welcome to Our Website!

Our website "The Film Nostalgia " is based on the desire of the people to watch the old movie's or recently released movie's again in the theatre's. This time the website is for college to show the movies through the movie club of the college . In this website we collect the DATA BASE of some famous movie's and put it on a list for voting .The targeted audience of the college first creates their account on the website and then, they started voting for the movie they want to watch on next show, we conduct the voting for approximately 4 to 5 days of the week and show the movie at the weekend .
